# PetNeeds.ai Email Signup Plan

Date: 2026-05-29

## Goal

Build an email list with a useful free pet safety guide and simple pet-care updates.

The first version should stay as planning content only. No live email tool should be connected until privacy, disclosure, testing, and form handling are ready.

## Lead magnet idea

Free guide title:

`Pet Safety Quick Guide for Families`

Simple promise:

A printable one-page guide that helps families write down pet details, organize concerns, and know what questions to ask local support.

## Audience

Primary visitors:

- Dog owners
- Cat owners
- New puppy families
- New kitten families
- Fish tank beginners
- Rescue pet families
- Senior pet owners
- Parents helping children care for pets

## Signup form fields

First version should ask only for:

- Email address
- Optional pet type

Do not ask for sensitive details in the first version.

## Email topics

Possible newsletter topics:

1. Weekly pet checklist
2. Puppy and kitten starter tips
3. Fish tank beginner tips
4. Senior pet comfort notes
5. Rescue pet first-week routines
6. Seasonal pet reminders
7. New guide announcements

## Trust notes

The signup form should explain:

- What the visitor will receive
- How often emails may arrive
- That they can unsubscribe
- That privacy terms apply

## Suggested first signup copy

Headline:

`Get the Free Pet Safety Quick Guide`

Body:

`A simple family checklist for writing down pet details, routines, and questions before choosing next steps.`

Button:

`Send Me the Guide`

## Future email tool options

Possible tools to compare later:

- MailerLite
- ConvertKit
- Brevo
- Mailchimp

Compare by:

- Free plan limits
- Ease of setup
- Form embed options
- Simple automation
- Cost as the list grows
- Compliance tools

## Build order

1. Keep homepage signup form as a visual placeholder.
2. Create or design the free guide.
3. Choose an email tool.
4. Update privacy page before going live.
5. Connect the form.
6. Test confirmation and unsubscribe flow.
7. Start with one simple welcome email.

## Do not add yet

- Live email form integration
- Tracking scripts
- Hidden form capture
- Payment tools
- Complex automation
- SMS collection
- Sensitive pet or visitor data collection
