Small Pet Home Setup

A simple guide for creating a calm, clean home space for small pets.

Home Setup

  • Quiet location
  • Clean bedding
  • Correct food
  • Fresh water
  • Safe hiding place

Family Routine

  • Check food daily
  • Refresh water
  • Clean on a schedule
  • Use gentle handling
  • Write down changes

Species Matter

Rabbits, hamsters, guinea pigs, and gerbils need different food, space, and handling. Keep notes by pet type.
