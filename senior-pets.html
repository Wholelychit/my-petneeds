Senior pet care

Helping Older Pets Stay Comfortable

Simple support ideas for aging pets and the families who care for them.

Important Reminder

Major changes in eating, walking, breathing, or behavior should be checked by a veterinarian.

Comfort

Soft bedding, easier movement, and calm routines may help senior pets feel safer and more relaxed.

Nutrition

Older pets sometimes need adjusted diets and hydration support.
