Healthy Cat Living

Nutrition, enrichment, and stress reduction.

Nutrition

Cats require high-protein diets with taurine for heart and eye health. Avoid toxic foods such as chocolate, onions, and garlic.

Behavior & Enrichment

Scratching posts, climbing structures, and interactive toys help reduce stress and destructive behaviors.

Health & Safety

Regular vet visits, parasite prevention, dental care, and safe indoor environments are essential.
