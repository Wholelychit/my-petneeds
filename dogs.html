How to Reduce Dog Anxiety

Start With Routine

Many dogs feel safer when meals, walks, play, and bedtime happen on a steady schedule.

Use Calm Training

Reward calm behavior. Avoid yelling because it can make fear worse.

Give Safe Space

A crate, bed, or quiet room can help a nervous dog rest away from noise.

Watch Sudden Changes

Pain, illness, age, or stress can look like behavior trouble. Write down what changed and when.
