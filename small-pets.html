Comfort Basics

Small pets do best with calm spaces, clean homes, correct food, and gentle handling close to the floor.

Family care

Small Pet Comfort Checklist

Quiet Space

Give small pets a calm area away from loud noise and rough handling.

Clean Home

Clean bedding, bowls, and homes on a steady schedule.

Correct Food

Species matter. Rabbits, guinea pigs, and hamsters do not eat the same diet.

Supervise Children

Teach children to use slow hands and calm voices.
