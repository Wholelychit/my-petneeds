Small Pet Comfort Checklist

Quiet Space

Small pets need calm areas away from loud noise and rough handling.

Clean Habitat

Clean bedding, bowls, and cages on a steady schedule.

Correct Food

Species matter. Rabbits, guinea pigs, and hamsters do not eat the same diet.

Supervise Children

Teach children to hold small pets gently and safely.
