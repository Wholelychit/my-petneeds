Fish Tank Starter Checklist

A simple starter checklist for families setting up a beginner fish tank.

Setup

  • Tank
  • Filter
  • Conditioned water
  • Thermometer
  • Food

Daily Routine

  • Feed lightly
  • Check water temperature
  • Watch fish activity
  • Check filter flow
  • Write down changes

Family Jobs

Pick one person to feed the fish so the tank is not overfed.
