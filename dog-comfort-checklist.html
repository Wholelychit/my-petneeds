Dog Comfort Checklist

Use this simple checklist when a dog seems unsettled or affected by a change in routine.

Check the Setting

  • Room temperature
  • Wet fur
  • Loud noises
  • New people or pets
  • Recent travel
  • Less rest than usual

Calm First Steps

  • Offer a quiet place
  • Keep voices calm
  • Use a steady routine
  • Write down what changed
  • Use the safety guide for bigger concerns
