Pet Safety Warning Signs

This guide helps families know when to stop searching online and contact a qualified professional.

Do Not Wait on Serious Signs

If a pet seems very weak, cannot breathe normally, has been injured, may have eaten something unsafe, or is acting extremely different, contact a veterinarian or emergency clinic.

Things to Write Down

  • Pet type, age, and weight if known
  • What happened and when it started
  • Food, medicine, plant, or chemical exposure
  • Any behavior, energy, breathing, or bathroom changes

Before You Travel

  • Call the clinic first when possible.
  • Bring packaging, labels, photos, or notes.
  • Keep the pet calm and safely contained.
  • Do not give human medicine unless a professional says to.
Educational only: PetNeeds.ai cannot diagnose or treat pets. Serious pet concerns need professional care.
